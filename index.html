Mission Statement

Washington State Tae Kwon Do Association is a non-profit public charity and our
mission is to provide a challenging environment that allows the development of the
physical, mental, and emotional self for any student regardless of age, gender, race, or
level of athletic ability. Our commitment is to the health, strength, and longevity of the
individual. Through tae kwon do training, an individual can strengthen their body and
their mind. We seek to make a positive contribution to our community through training
and student/teacher interaction.
The funds raised by the WSTA via seminars and fundraisers will go back to our college-bound black belts as scholarships to attend their choice of universities.

We, as a team, strive to:
  • Assist and address the wants and needs of our students and families.
  • Provide a safe environment to educate and train students and families with the tools to protect themselves with self-defense tactics and maintain optimal health.
  • Provide continuous education and training for individuals and instructors to maintain and update skills in communication and instruction.
  • Provide opportunities for underprivileged individuals and families to be able to participate and be a part of our team.
  • Maintain an environment that gives each individual the opportunity to pursue and achieve a greater level of personal success and freedom.
  • Maintain a commitment to excellence.
